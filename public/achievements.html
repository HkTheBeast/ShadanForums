Shadan College of Engineering Technology Achievements

Shadan College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) has achieved significant milestones since its establishment in 1995. Here are some of the key achievements

ACHIEVEMENTS

The Shadan College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) is a private university located in Peerancheru, Himayat Sagar Road, Hyderabad, India. It was founded in 1994 by the Shadan Educational Society formed by Vizarath Rasool Khan. The college is permitted by the Government of Telangana and approved by AICTE, affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH).

ACADEMIC ACHIEVEMENTS

1.UNIVERSITY RANKS AND GOLD MEDALS

i]Multiple SCET students have secured JNTUH university ranks across departments, particularly in Computer Science Engineering (CSE) and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE).

ii]Several students have received JNTUH Gold Medals for academic excellence (Top 1% in university).

2.ACCREDIATION STATUS

i]Achieved NBA accreditation for selected engineering branches (periodic renewals).

ii]Certified with NAAC accreditation for institutional quality standards.

iii]ISO 9001:2015 certification for academic and administrative quality management.

3.RESEARCH AND INNOVATION

i]Faculty published 300+ research papers in national and international journals (IEEE, Springer, Scopus).

ii]Established Research & Development (R&D) Cell for faculty and student innovation.

iii]Collaborated with DRDO, ISRO, and AICTE for minor funded projects and student prototype development.
