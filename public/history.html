Founding & Background


Establishment: Shadan College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) was established in 1995 by the Shadan Educational Society (SES).

Founder: The society was founded under the guidance of Dr. Mohd Vizarath Rasool Khan, an educationist who led several initiatives to promote minority education.

Location: SCET is located on Himayath Sagar Road, Peerancheru, Hyderabad, Telangana, India.

Significance: It is a minority engineering college and was the first minority engineering college affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH) in 1995.


About Dr. Mohammed Vizarath Rasool Khan


Profile: Dr. Mohammed Vizarath Rasool Khan is a renowned educationist and philanthropist who founded the Shadan Educational Society (SES) to promote minority and underprivileged education in Hyderabad.

Contribution: Under his guidance, SES established the Shadan College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) in 1995, the first minority engineering college affiliated with JNTUH.

Vision: Dr. Rasool Khan‚Äôs vision emphasizes quality technical education, research, and student empowerment, enabling thousands of students to succeed academically and professionally while contributing to societal development.



Early Years & Growth

Initial Courses: At its inception, SCET started with three B.Tech courses: Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), and Mechanical Engineering (ME), with an intake of about 140 students.

Expansion: Over the years, the college expanded its programs significantly, adding branches such as Civil, Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE), Information Technology (IT), and more recent specializations like Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science.


Accreditation & Recognitions


Approval: SCET is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated with JNTUH.

Accreditations: The college has also received accreditations from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).


Recent Status


Programs Offered: Today, SCET offers a broad spectrum of undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA) programs, with approximately 8 undergraduate and 7 postgraduate programs and a total intake of around 792 students.

Alumni: The institution has produced over 10,000 engineers working in India and abroad.


Vision & Mission Emphasis


Educational Approach: The college emphasizes outcome-based education, student-centric learning, modern infrastructure including laboratories, libraries, and computing facilities, and strong industry linkages.

Events & Student Life Highlights


Annual & Special Events: SCET organizes a wide variety of technical, cultural, and social events throughout the year. Students participate in activities such as national-level technical fests, cybersecurity and ethical-hacking workshops, overseas-education awareness seminars, anti-drug abuse rallies, annual cultural festivals, mathematics week, energy conservation week, voter-awareness programs, and plantation drives under the NSS unit. Facilities like the auditorium, gyms, laboratories, and hostels support these events, ensuring a vibrant campus life.

Clubs & Student Bodies: The college has a structured system of clubs and student councils covering multiple domains including technical, cultural, sports, eco-club, women empowerment, and health. Extracurricular opportunities include:

  • Technical Clubs: Coding, robotics, electronics, and innovation-focused initiatives.
  • Cultural Clubs: Music, dance, drama, and fine arts.
  • Sports & Fitness: Indoor and outdoor games, gym facilities, and annual sports competitions.
  • Student Welfare Committees: Eco-club (Green Campus / Haritha Haram), clean-campus initiatives, and health-focused programs.

Active cycles of clubs and departmental student groups organize frequent events, providing students with continuous opportunities for engagement and leadership. Weekly club activities, monthly events, and departmental programs create an active, inclusive, and dynamic campus culture.

Holistic Student-Life Support: Beyond academics, SCET offers infrastructure and facilities that support a well-rounded student experience, including libraries, hostels, transport, and modern laboratories. This combination of academic programs, extracurricular clubs, cultural and sports activities, and social initiatives fosters a holistic learning environment for all students.
