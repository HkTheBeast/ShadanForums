Take Control of Everything and Everyone in Your Classroom!

With SVLENT, you can effortlessly manage every aspect of your classroom

SVLENT is the dedicated platform designed to give teachers seamless control over the pulse of their classroom. Built to streamline your workflow, it consolidates everything from daily attendance and assignment tracking to performance analytics and student behavior notes into one intuitive, centralized hub. By centralizing these essential tasks, SVLENT replaces scattered systems and paper trails with clarity and efficiency, allowing you to reclaim time and focus on what truly matters—connecting with and guiding your students.

From the moment you begin, you gain complete, organized oversight. Mark daily attendance in seconds and maintain clear, automatic records. Track assignment completion in real-time to instantly see who is on track and who may need support. Log marks and scores to build a comprehensive academic profile for each learner, and go beyond grades by tracking custom performance levels and rating students against specific skills or rubrics to capture a full picture of their growth.

SVLENT empowers you to support the complete development of every student. The platform's structured annotation system allows you to formally flag areas of concern to document and manage needed interventions. Conversely, you can spotlight achievements and positive contributions to encourage a supportive classroom environment. To keep your own workflow smooth, a built-in task manager helps you organize your responsibilities and reminders right alongside your student data, ensuring your entire classroom ecosystem is synchronized.
