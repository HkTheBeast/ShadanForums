What is Svlent?

One place for
everything that matters
in your classroom.

Svlent is a dedicated teacher's platform that consolidates daily attendance, assignment tracking, marks, record submissions, student highlights, and warnings into a single, organized dashboard. No more scattered spreadsheets. No more paper.

Every student gets a full profile. Every action is saved instantly. You stay in control of your classroom without drowning in admin.
