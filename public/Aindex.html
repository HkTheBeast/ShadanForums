What is Svlent?

One place for
everything that matters
in your classroom.

Svlent is a dedicated teacher's platform that consolidates daily attendance, assignment tracking, marks, record submissions, student highlights, warnings, and a full teaching planner into a single, organized dashboard. No more scattered spreadsheets. No more paper.

Every student gets a full profile with a live composite score. Every action is saved instantly. You stay in control of your classroom without drowning in admin.

How It Works

Three steps to a fully organized classroom.

01

Add Your Students

Create student profiles with roll numbers, names, and optional photos. Your class roster is ready in minutes.

02

Track Daily

Take attendance each day, toggle assignment completion, update marks, and log record submissions as they come in.

03

Plan & Teach

Use the Teaching Planner to structure your curriculum, track progress trackers, and manage your weekly timetable.

Everything Included

Built for how teachers actually work.

Student Profiles

Every student gets a rich profile card — roll number, name, and optional photo. A live composite score (graded S, A, B, C, or D) is calculated automatically from their attendance, submissions, marks, and behaviour, giving you an instant snapshot of each student's overall standing.

Photo · Composite Score · Grade S→D

Attendance Tracking

Mark the whole class as Present, Late, or Absent in under a minute. Every session is saved to the database, and each student's running attendance percentage is calculated and displayed automatically. View full date-by-date history for any student at any time.

Present / Late / Absent Running % Full History

Assignment Tracking

Track two separate assignments per student. Each is a simple toggle — done or pending — so you can scan the entire class list in seconds and know exactly who's submitted and who hasn't, without opening any extra screens.

Assignment 1 & 2 Done / Pending

Marks & Grades

Enter marks for four separate assessments per student: Mid-Term 1, Mid-Term 2, Internal Lab, and External Lab. Svlent auto-totals all four into a combined score out of 160 and displays it directly on the student card. A class-wide average bar lets you see how the whole group is performing at a glance.

Mid 1 & Mid 2 Internal Lab External Lab Auto Total /160 Class Average

Record Submissions

Track whether each student has submitted their Record Book, Observation Book, and PPT. Toggle each item independently per student. Students who have submitted everything get a gold "all done" border so you can spot completions instantly.

Record Book Obs. Book PPT

Student Highlights

Star any student to mark them as a top performer. Their profile card glows gold across the whole platform. Use this to recognise consistent effort, exceptional work, or any student deserving special attention — visible in the dedicated Highlights page.

Gold Glow · Dedicated Page

Formal Warnings

Issue up to 3 formal warnings per student directly from their profile. Warning dots fill up visually as they accumulate. At 3 warnings the card turns red with a pulsing alert — a clear, unambiguous signal that disciplinary action may be needed.

1–3 Warnings · Red Alert at Max

Teaching Planner

A dedicated planning workspace that lives alongside your student data. Build your full curriculum with Subjects, Topics, and Subtopics — tick them off as you teach and watch your progress update in real time. Set up custom Progress Trackers (classes completed, syllabus coverage %, chapters done) with a visual ring and stepper controls. Map out your entire week in the color-coded Weekly Timetable with subject, section, time, and room for every slot.

Progress Trackers Curriculum Builder Topics & Subtopics Weekly Timetable Section Filters Color-Coded
