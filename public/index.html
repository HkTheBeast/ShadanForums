🌟 Shadan College of Engineering & Technology

🏛️ Established in 1995 under the aegis of the Shadan Educational Society, Shadan College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) in Hyderabad has grown steadily into a premier institution for technical education in Telangana... more

🎯 Our Mission

To create competent professionals with strong ethical values who can contribute to society through innovation and excellence in engineering and technology.

From its inception as the first minority-engineering college affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH) in 1995, SCET has consistently combined rigorous academic programmes with hands-on learning experiences.

The college offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering and technology—spanning Civil, Mechanical, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics & Communication, Computer Science, and emerging fields like Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.

These disciplines are complemented by state-of-the-art labs, modern workshops, and advanced infrastructure designed to support practical learning and foster innovation. Situated on Himayat Sagar Road, Peerancheru, our campus provides a focused environment for study while maintaining excellent connectivity with the city.

🏆 Key Achievements

  • 🎖️ NAAC Accredited Institution
  • 🔬 Advanced Research Facilities
  • 🌍 Industry-Academia Collaboration
  • 💡 Innovation & Incubation Center
  • 📚 JNTUH Affiliated

SCET places strong emphasis on holistic development: along with technical competencies, we foster student engagement through seminars, technical events, hackathons, and co-curricular activities. With over two decades of experience in engineering education, the institution continuously evolves its curriculum and facilities to meet dynamic industry demands.

In summary, SCET stands as a well-established engineering college in Hyderabad that masterfully blends academic depth, practical training, and a responsive learning environment—making it the ideal choice for aspiring engineers seeking both foundational knowledge and real-world readiness.
